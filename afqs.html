FAQs

Find answers to common questions about our Vector Art, Digitizing, and Patch services — all crafted with care, accuracy, and professional expertise.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Pre-Order Questions

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Payoneer, PayPal, and major credit/debit cards. Our payment options are designed to support global clients and make ordering easy and secure.
Do you offer discounts for bulk orders?
Yes, we offer special discounts on bulk orders for embroidery digitizing and vector art files. Contact our support team for a custom quote.

General Questions

Is it recommended to submit my order via email?
We recommend placing orders directly through our website portal for faster assignment to a skilled artist. Email submission is optional.
What are your operational hours?
Our team operates 24/7, ensuring clients from all time zones receive fast responses and timely delivery.
Can you work with rough artwork or replicate a design from a garment?
Absolutely! We can work with rough sketches, low-resolution images, or replicate designs from garments.
What is the process for uploading my artwork?
Upload artwork through our website portal or via email. Accepted formats: JPG, PNG, PDF, EPS, AI, CDR, PSD.
How can I confirm if my artwork meets required standards?
Our experts review uploaded artwork and guide you if adjustments are needed before processing.
What is the expected timeframe for completing digitization or vector files?
Most standard orders are completed within 12–24 hours. Rush service is available upon request.
What file formats are accepted for artwork?
We accept JPG, PNG, PDF, AI, CDR, EPS, and PSD formats for artwork submissions.
Which embroidery formats are available for selection?
We provide digitized files in DST, PES, JEF, EXP, VP3, XXX, and HUS formats. Vector artwork is delivered in AI, EPS, SVG, and PDF formats.
How do you guarantee the quality of your work?
Each file goes through a strict manual quality-check process to ensure your embroidery and vector files are production-ready.
How will I be notified when my file is ready for download?
You will receive an email notification and can also download completed files directly from the client portal.
